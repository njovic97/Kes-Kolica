We are glad you decided to try our application!

The following will explain in detail how the application works and how to get started with using it. To get started you need a phone with iOS or Android operating system. If you own one you can continue reading the text ..

1. Download and install the app

On your phone, open the play store (app store) and enter the name of our application (Kes Kolica) in the search box. When you find it, download it and install it on your phone. Or if you browse our site over the phone by simply clicking on the "Download" button you can do all this.

2. Register

When you have installed the application and launched it in the upper right corner you will notice a "Register" button. Clicking on it gives you a series of fields that you need to fill out to create an account. The information must be accurate and it is advisable to enter an e-mail address that you can access.

3. Login

After successful registration, by pressing the "Login" button, you will receive fields for entering e-mail and password. Fill them in with the information you previously entered during registration and click the "Login" button again.

4. A short guide through the application

Now that you are a member and user of our application, it is our turn to explain where, how and what. By clicking on the "My Account" button, you can see your basic information as well as the balance on your account. Pressing the "QR" button starts the camera (scanner) for the QR code located on the cart. Point the camera at the code and it will be read and the cart recorded and tracking activated. Pressing the "Locate" button displays the location of your strollers in the market as well as additional information on how far you are from the shelves, whether they are on the move, and near which products they are located. Clicking on the "Vouchers" button opens a page where you can see all your vouchers that you have collected using the application as well as the benefits and discounts that are in progress. In addition to each action with which we have a partnership, the percentage of discounts that you can get using the application will be listed.
